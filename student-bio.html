CONDENSER OF THE CHILLER IS COOLED BY COOLING TOWER, HENCE KNOWN AS WATER CHILLER PLANT. CHILLERS GENERATE COOL WATER TO PROVIDE AIR CONDITIONING TO BUILDINGS CHILLED WATER (6C) IS PRODUCED IN THE EVAPORATOR OF THE CHILLER AND WATER (12C) COMING FROM AHUS TO THE EVAPORATOR WILL RELEASE THE HEAT TO THE CONDENSOR VIA REFRIGERANT. EVAPORATOR AND CONDENSOR ARE COMPLETELY ISOLATED FROM EACH OTHER. CONDENSED WATER(32C) ( WATER THAT HAS PICKED UP HEAT) WILL BE TRANSFERRED BACK TO THE COOLING TOWER WITH THE USE OF A PUMP FANS INSIDE THE COOLING TOWER DISSIPATE THE EXCESSIVE HEAT AND RETURNS ROOM TEMPERATURE WATER BACK (27C) TO THE CONDENSOR THIS WATER WILL AGAIN PICK UP HEAT FROM THE WATER COMING BACK FROM THE AHUS TO THE EVAPORATOR VIA REFRIGERANT. AND THE CYCLE CONTINUES.
